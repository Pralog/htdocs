Failiure is Not the End, It is a Gate to Learn Something New


Want to know how to develop Websites without WIX or Dreamweaver? Want to develop simple websites without buying or dowloading expensive and different softwares. You don't have to worry anymore. If you do not have the necessary tools to develop a website, you've came to the right place. Gitworks is an organisation which focuses on helping people developing their own websites using nothing more than NotePad and NotePad++. You do not need a software like Dreamweaver or use anything like WIX to develop simple and creative websites. You can learn the language and how to do it all by yourselves. This is Gitworks, to learn and to develop simple but creative sites without having to use external code generating softwares and place anything you want freely on your site without a problem.
